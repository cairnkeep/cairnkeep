<objective>
Upgrade the round-trip stage's assertions and transport so the reliability
claim is credible: replace substring greps with genuine NDJSON tool-event
parsing (D-08/D-09), drive the stage through opencode serve/`--attach`
(D-11), and split the retry so it absorbs infra flakiness only, never masks a
narration failure, logging the retry count for the soak (D-13).

Purpose: A 5/5 soak (Plan 02) built on substring greps could be five false
positives. This plan makes each PASS mean a real tool call happened and the
canary actually round-tripped — the two halves that make one credible claim.
Output: `scripts/lib/assert-tool-event.mjs`, its offline fixture test, and a
hardened `run_stage_remember_recall`.
</objective>
