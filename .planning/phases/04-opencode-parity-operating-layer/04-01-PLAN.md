<objective>
De-risk OCP-05 before committing to the full `memory-wakeup.ts` rewrite. Research (04-RESEARCH.md, Pitfall 1 / Open Question 1 / Assumption A4) flags that OpenCode's `experimental.chat.system.transform` mutations to `output.system` may be silently discarded by the runtime (GH `anomalyco/opencode#17100`, closed not-planned) — the exact hook the existing wakeup plugin already depends on. If that bug reproduces against the installed OpenCode CLI, a perfect D-04 reimplementation still surfaces nothing to the model and OCP-05's acceptance test fails for a reason unrelated to the port.

This plan runs a throwaway probe against the live `opencode` CLI to settle three questions and records a channel decision that gates Plan 04-03.

Purpose: Choose the wakeup injection channel from evidence, not assumption, and confirm the `tool.execute.before` file-path field and `client.session.messages()` shape that Plans 04-04/04-05 rely on.
Output: `.planning/phases/04-opencode-parity-operating-layer/04-SPIKE-INJECTION.md` recording the probe results and the chosen channel.
</objective>
